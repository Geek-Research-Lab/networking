## Differential Geometry
***
Pre-requisites: Multivariable Calculus
***
```
Graphs and level sets of functions on Euclidean spaces, 
vector fields, 
integral curves of vector fields, 
tangent spaces. 

Surfaces in Euclidean spaces, 
vector fields on surfaces, 
orientation, 
Gauss map.

Geodesics, 
parallel transport, 
Weingarten map.

Curvature of plane curves, 
arc length and line integrals.

Curvature of surfaces.

Parametrized surfaces, 
local equivalence of surfaces.

Gauss-Bonnet Theorem, 
Poincare-Hopf Index Theorem. 
```
