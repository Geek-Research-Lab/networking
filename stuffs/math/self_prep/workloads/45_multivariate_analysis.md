## Multivariate Analysis
***
Pre-requisites: Statistical Inference
***
```
K-variate normal distribution. 
Estimation of the mean vector and dispersion matrix. 
Random sampling from multivariate normal distribution. 
Multivariate distribution theory. 
Discriminant and canonical analysis. 
Factor analysis. 
Principal components.

Distribution theory associated with the analysis.
```
