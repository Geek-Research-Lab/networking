## Operators on Hilbert Spaces
***
Pre-requistes: Functional Analysis
***
```
Adjoints of bounded operators on a Hilbert space, 
Normal, self-adjoint and unitary operators, 
their spectra and numerical ranges.

Compact operators on Hilbert spaces. 
Spectral theorem for compact self-adjoint operators. 

Application to Sturm-Liouville Problems.
```
