## Spectral Approximation
***
Pre-requistes: Functional Analysis
***
```
Spectral decomposition. 
Spectral sets of finite type. 
Adjoint and product spaces.

Convergence of operators: 
norm, 
collectively compact and n convergence. 
Error estimates.

Finite rank approximations based on projections and approximations for integral operators. 

A posteriori error estimates.

Matrix formulations for finite rank operators.

Iterative refinement of a simple eigenvalue.

Numerical examples.
```
