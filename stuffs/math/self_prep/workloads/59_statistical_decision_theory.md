## Statistical Decision Theory
***
Pre-requisites: Statistical Inference
***
```
Decision functions, 
Risk functions, 
utility and subjective probability, 
Randomization, 

Optimal decision rules. 
Admissibility and completeness, 
Existence of Bayes Decision Rules, 
Existence of a Minimal complete class, 
Essential completeness of the class of non-randomized rules. 
The minimax theorem.

Invariant statistical decision problems. 
Multiple decision problems.

Sequential decision problems.
```
