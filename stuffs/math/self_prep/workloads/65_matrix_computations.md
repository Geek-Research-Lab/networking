## Matrix Computations
```
Basic iterative methods for solutions of linear systems and their rates of convergence. 
Generalized conjugate gradient, 
Krybov space and Lanczos methods. 
Iterative methods for symmetric, 
non-symmetric and generalized eigenvalue problems. 
Singular value decompositions. 
Fast computations for structured matrices. 
Polynomial matrix computations. 
Perturbation bounds for eigenvalues.
```
