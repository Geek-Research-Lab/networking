## Mathematical Methods
***
Pre-requistes: Partial Differential Equations
***
```
Asymptotic expansions, 
Watson's lemma, 
method of stationary phase and saddle point method. 
Applications to differential equations. 
Behaviour of solutions near an irregular singular point, 
Stoke's phenomenon. 
Method of strained coordinates and matched asymptotic expansions.

Variational principles, 
Lax-Milgram theorem and applications to boundary value problems.

Calculus of variations and integral equations. 
Volterra integral equations of first and second kind. 
Iterative methods and Neumann series.
```
