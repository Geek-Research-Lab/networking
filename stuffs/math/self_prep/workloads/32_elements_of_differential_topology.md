## Elements of Differential Topology
***
Pre-requisites: Multivariable Calculus
***
```
Differentiable Manifolds in Rn: 
Review of inverse and implicit function theorems; 
tangent spaces and tangent maps; 
immersions; 
submersions and embeddings.

Regular Values: 
Regular and critical values; 
regular inverse image theorem; 
Sard's theorem; Morse lemma.

Transversality: 
Orientations of manifolds; 
oriented and mod 2 intersection numbers; 
degree of maps. 
Application to Fundamental theorem of Algebra.

*Lefschetz theory of vector fields and flows: 
Poincare-Hopf index theorem; 
Gauss-Bonnet theorem.

*Abstract manifolds: 
Examples such as real and complex projective spaces and Grassmannian varieties; 
Whitney embedding theorems.
```
